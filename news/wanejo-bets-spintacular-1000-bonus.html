Wanejo Bets Launches Spintacular 1000: R50 Deposit Gets You R50 Bonus and 200 Free Spins

Promos Operator Promotion 22 March 2026 2 min read By Lerato Dlamini
📰Promos

Wanejo Bets has launched a casino deposit bonus called Spintacular 1000. The offer gives new players a 100% match on their first deposit (minimum R50, maximum R1,000) along with 200 free spins on selected Pragmatic Play slots. It runs until 30 April 2026.

How the bonus works

The structure is straightforward. Deposit at least R50 and Wanejo Bets matches it 100%, up to R1,000. On top of that, you get 200 free spins on Pragmatic Play's 1000-series games. So a R50 deposit gives you R50 in bonus funds plus the 200 spins, while a R1,000 deposit maxes out the match at R1,000 plus the same 200 spins.

The bonus must be activated within 48 hours of your deposit. If you don't activate it in that window, you lose it.

Which games qualify

The free spins are restricted to Pragmatic Play's 1000-series titles. Based on the promotional material, the qualifying games include:

  • Gates of Olympus 1000
  • Sweet Bonanza 1000
  • Sugar Rush 1000
  • Olympus X1000
  • Big Bass Bonanza 1000
  • Big Bass Splash 1000
  • Starlight Princess 1000
  • Wisdom of Athena 1000
  • Lucky Tiger 1000

These are all high-volatility slots with multiplier mechanics. They're popular titles, so most regular casino players will already know them.

Payout cap and wagering

The maximum payout from bonus funds is R1,000. Any winnings above that amount are forfeited. Standard wagering requirements apply, and the bonus amount itself can't be withdrawn as cash.

Wanejo Bets reserves the right to void bonuses or winnings if it detects abuse, duplicate accounts, or any breach of the promotional terms.

Daily mystery prizes

On top of the deposit bonus, the Spintacular 1000 promotion includes three daily mystery prizes. The promotional page doesn't specify exactly what these are, but they're awarded daily throughout the campaign period.

Promotion dates

The offer opened on 16 March 2026 and closes on 30 April 2026 at 23:59 SAST. It's available to new players on the Wanejo Bets casino platform.

Key terms

  • Minimum deposit: R50
  • Match bonus: 100% up to R1,000
  • Free spins: 200 on Pragmatic Play 1000-series slots
  • Maximum payout from bonus: R1,000
  • Activation window: 48 hours from deposit
  • Promotion period: 16 March to 30 April 2026
  • One bonus per customer, household, IP address, or device

Responsible gambling

Set a deposit limit before claiming any bonus. If gambling stops being fun, contact the SA Responsible Gambling Foundation on 0800 006 008 or visit responsiblegambling.org.za. You must be 18 or older to participate.
