Pantherbet Launches Virtual Sports with 24/7 Betting Markets

Features 05 March 2026 1 min read By Lerato Dlamini

Pantherbet has launched a virtual sports section featuring football, horse racing, greyhound racing, and motorsport. Events run around the clock with results every 2-3 minutes - no fixture lists, no off-seasons.

What is on offer

Virtual football is the standout, with simulated league matches using realistic odds and stats. Horse racing and greyhounds follow the standard virtual format - short races with fixed odds available right up to the off. Motorsport is the newest addition, offering head-to-head and podium finish markets.

Graphics are decent without being spectacular. The animations are functional rather than cinematic, but for bettors who care about odds rather than visuals, that is fine.

Who is this for?

Virtual sports fill the gap when there is nothing live to bet on. Late at night, midweek, during international breaks - there is always something running. They also suit bettors who want quick-fire action without waiting 90 minutes for a football result.

Betway has the most polished virtual sports offering in SA, while Gbets and PlayaBets also carry a solid selection. Pantherbet's entry to the space adds another option, though the range is still narrower than the bigger operators.

A word on virtual sports

The outcomes are generated by RNG (random number generators), not based on real-world form. Past results do not predict future ones. Treat them as casino-style entertainment with sports packaging, not as something you can analyse and beat through research.
