About Ozow Instant EFT

Ozow Instant EFT is a South African payment gateway that enables automated, real-time electronic funds transfers directly from a user's bank account without cards. Users select their bank, log in via secure redirect, auto-populate payment details, and confirm with 2FA OTP from their bank. Funds transfer instantly to the merchant upon approval.

Min Deposit

R10 - R50

Max Deposit

R100,000 - R500,000 per transaction (site-dependent)

How to Deposit Using Ozow Instant EFT

1

Log into your bookmaker account and go to Deposits.

2

Select "Ozow Instant EFT" as your payment method.

3

Pick your bank from the list.

4

You will be redirected to your bank's secure login page.

5

Log in and confirm the payment via OTP or push notification.

6

Your funds are credited instantly.
