About Zapper

Zapper is a free South African 'scan to pay' mobile app owned by the Payfast network, allowing users to link credit/debit/cheque cards and make quick QR code payments. Users download the app, add card details securely, and scan merchant QR codes for seamless transactions without sharing info directly. It's designed for convenience, security, and rewards like loyalty points.

Min Deposit

Max Deposit

5

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Instant deposits and fast withdrawals
  • no fees
  • highly secure (card details not shared with bookmaker, encrypted transactions via secure gateway)
  • easy QR scan process
  • reduced fraud risk
  • SA-specific with rewards/loyalty perks

Cons

  • Linked to personal cards so requires bankroll management to avoid overspending
  • limited to bookies with Payfast integration
  • not as widespread as EFT or vouchers yet

Security

Transactions via secure payment gateway with encryption; card details tokenized/stored only in app, never shared with merchants/bookmakers; 3D Secure support; PCI-DSS compliant; fraud prevention built-in
