About Blu Voucher

Blu Voucher is a prepaid voucher launched in 2020 by Blue Label Distribution for South Africans to make secure online payments without a bank account. Users purchase it with cash at retailers for a chosen amount and receive a 16-digit PIN code to redeem at partner sites. It provides access to services like gaming, airtime, and more in ZAR.

Max Deposit

How to Deposit Using Blu Voucher

1

Purchase a Blu Voucher at any participating retailer or online.

2

Receive your unique PIN code via SMS or printed receipt.

3

Log into your bookmaker account and navigate to Deposits.

4

Select "Blu Voucher" as your deposit method.

5

Enter your PIN code and the deposit amount.

6

Confirm the transaction. Funds are credited instantly.

Pros and Cons

Pros
