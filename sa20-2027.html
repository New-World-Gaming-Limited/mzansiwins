What Is SA20?

SA20 is South Africa's premier T20 franchise cricket league. It was launched by Cricket South Africa in January 2023 and has grown rapidly into one of the world's top domestic T20 competitions. Each of the six teams is owned by an IPL franchise, which brings financial muscle, international player access, and credibility that most new leagues take years to build.

The SA20 window sits in January and February, which coincides with the quieter part of the IPL auction cycle and allows the league to attract world-class talent. Players from the IPL's global network compete alongside South Africa's best domestic players, producing high-quality T20 cricket across six distinctive venues.

The format is straightforward: 6 teams each play the other 5 twice in the group stage, producing 30 matches. The top four qualify for the playoffs, which follow a two-qualifier structure. The two best sides in the group play Qualifier 1, the winner going directly to the final. The loser of that game gets another shot through Qualifier 2, where they face the winner of the Eliminator (played between the 3rd and 4th placed teams). The final brings together the winners of Qualifier 1 and Qualifier 2.

Teams

All six SA20 franchises are owned by IPL teams, giving each a direct pipeline into the IPL's global player network. Here's a profile of each club.

Sunrisers Eastern Cape
2026 Champions | 3x winners
The most successful team in SA20 history. Sunrisers Eastern Cape have won three of the first four tournaments, including the 2026 title against Pretoria Capitals at Newlands. Owned by the Sunrisers Hyderabad franchise under Kavya Maran, they play their home matches at St George's Park in Gqeberha. Their bowling depth, particularly Anrich Nortje and Marco Jansen, has been the cornerstone of their success. They enter 2027 as early favourites despite the target on their back.
Home: St George's Park, Gqeberha IPL Owner: Sunrisers Hyderabad (Kavya Maran)
MI Cape Town
2025 Champions
Owned by Mumbai Indians, MI Cape Town won their maiden SA20 title in 2025. Their home at Newlands Cricket Ground in Cape Town is one of the most iconic venues in world cricket. The Mumbai Indians' resources and scouting network give MI Cape Town one of the deepest squads in the competition. They were runners-up in the 2026 final and will be motivated to reclaim the title in 2027.
Home: Newlands, Cape Town IPL Owner: Mumbai Indians
Joburg Super Kings
CSK Backed
Owned by Chennai Super Kings, the Joburg Super Kings play out of The Wanderers in Johannesburg. They carry the CSK brand's reputation for experienced, cricketing-smart squad building. The Wanderers is a high-scoring venue where big totals are common, giving JSK's batting-heavy approach a natural advantage at home. They have yet to win the title but have been competitive in each edition.
Home: The Wanderers, Johannesburg IPL Owner: Chennai Super Kings
Pretoria Capitals
2026 Runners-up
Backed by Delhi Capitals, Pretoria Capitals play at SuperSport Park in Centurion, a ground known for its pace-friendly conditions early in matches. They were runners-up in both 2023 and 2026, showing consistent playoff quality. Sherfane Rutherford was their standout batter in 2026, and Dewald Brevis became the first player to score a SA20 final century in their most recent campaign. A genuine title contender.
Home: SuperSport Park, Centurion IPL Owner: Delhi Capitals
Paarl Royals
Rajasthan Royals
Owned by Rajasthan Royals, Paarl Royals play at Boland Park in Paarl, in the Cape Winelands. The ground's smaller dimensions and the wind can make it a lottery for bowlers, producing some of the highest-scoring matches in the competition. The Royals' franchise philosophy of developing young talent gives Paarl Royals a different character to some of the power-franchise sides, with depth in spin bowling a consistent feature.
Home: Boland Park, Paarl IPL Owner: Rajasthan Royals
Durban Super Giants
Lucknow Backed
Owned by Lucknow Super Giants, Durban Super Giants play at Kingsmead in Durban. The seaside ground offers unique conditions, with the sea breeze affecting swing and trajectory in ways that catch visiting teams off guard. DSG have the least silverware of the six franchises so far, but Kingsmead's home-ground advantage can be decisive, and their squad has improved with each season. KL Rahul's connection to the franchise adds pulling power for international talent.
Home: Kingsmead, Durban IPL Owner: Lucknow Super Giants

SA20 Betting Markets

SA20 betting markets are well-developed at all major SA bookmakers. Here's a guide to the main options, from the straightforward to the more niche.

Tournament Winner Season-Long
Back a team to lift the SA20 trophy. Markets open in the weeks before the tournament and update with in-running prices as the group stage progresses. Sunrisers Eastern Cape will open as favourites given their record, but their prices will be short. Value lies in finding the team most likely to upset them in the playoffs, where the two-game format for the top two sides creates extra elimination risk.
Opens: 4-6 weeks pre-tournament Best angle: Second or third favourite
Match Winner Core Market
The bread and butter of SA20 betting. Back one of two teams to win a specific match. Toss outcome matters significantly in T20 cricket, particularly at venues like Kingsmead and The Wanderers where chasing or setting can be markedly different propositions. Check pitch reports and recent results at each venue before betting match winners in SA20.
Toss impact: Significant Venue: Key factor
Top Batsman Player Prop
Bet on which player will score the most runs in the tournament. This market is one of the most unpredictable in cricket because batting positions and team tactics shift mid-tournament. Openers accumulate more runs by default in T20 cricket, but a middle-order player on a strong team that reaches the final can overtake a group-stage run-scorer. Look for players in settled batting positions on teams likely to make the playoffs.
Best position: Opener or No. 3 Typical winner: 300-500 runs
Top Bowler Player Prop
Bet on which bowler will take the most wickets across the tournament. SA20 pitches tend to offer more for pace than spin in the first half of matches, making pace-bowling depth important. Anrich Nortje and Kagiso Rabada have featured prominently in bowling honours in past seasons. A bowler on a team that reaches the final will have 2-3 extra matches in which to accumulate wickets.
Best option: Pace bowlers Playoff bonus: Extra games
Total Runs (Match) Match Market
Bet on whether the combined runs in a match will exceed or fall below a bookmaker-set line. SA20 matches at The Wanderers in Johannesburg and Boland Park in Paarl historically produce higher totals than matches at Kingsmead or St George's Park. The ground-specific nature of this market makes it one of the more approachable for bettors who have studied venue trends.
High-scoring venues: Wanderers, Boland Park Lower-scoring: Kingsmead, St George's
Man of the Match High Risk
Predict which player will be named Man of the Match in a specific game. This is a high-variance bet because a single outstanding performance by any player can decide the award, regardless of pre-match form. It's most useful as a same-game multi component when combined with a match winner bet, rather than as a standalone wager. Odds are typically generous because the field is wide open.
Odds range: 6/1 to 25/1 Best use: Accumulator leg
Player Props Match-Level
SA20 betting has expanded into individual player performance markets at most major bookmakers. These include over/under on a batsman's runs in a specific match, over/under on a bowler's wickets, first wicket method (caught, LBW, bowled), and first boundary. Player props are available primarily at Betway and Hollywoodbets, and they become richer in coverage as the tournament progresses. Starting lineup confirmation before markets open is essential for prop betting, since a player not in the XI is an immediate dead loss on any batting or bowling prop.
Availability: Betway, Hollywoodbets primarily Key rule: Confirm team sheets first Best match-ups: Group stage, knockout games

When markets do open, Sunrisers Eastern Cape are likely to be priced between 2/1 and 3/1 as three-time champions. MI Cape Town, as the only other title winner, should be next in line at around 3/1 to 5/1. Pretoria Capitals, who have been runners-up twice, and Joburg Super Kings typically follow, with Paarl Royals and Durban Super Giants available at longer prices for bettors who want a contrarian position.

Ante-post betting on SA20 carries one key risk: player availability is not confirmed until team sheets and auction results are known. A team that loses a key international player between the market opening and the tournament start can drift significantly. Betting later, once squads are confirmed, reduces this uncertainty at the cost of slightly shorter prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SA20 cricket?

SA20 is South Africa's premier T20 franchise cricket league, launched in 2023. Six teams, each owned by an IPL franchise, compete in a tournament held in January and February each year. The league is backed by Cricket South Africa and has grown rapidly in both viewership and global interest since its inaugural season.

When does SA20 Season 5 (2027) take place?

SA20 Season 5 is expected in January-February 2027. The exact schedule will be announced by Cricket South Africa in late 2026. This page will be updated with confirmed dates as soon as they are published.

Which teams play in SA20?

Six teams play in the SA20: Sunrisers Eastern Cape (owned by SRH), MI Cape Town (Mumbai Indians), Joburg Super Kings (CSK), Pretoria Capitals (Delhi Capitals), Paarl Royals (Rajasthan Royals), and Durban Super Giants (Lucknow Super Giants). All six are owned by Indian Premier League franchises.

Who won SA20 in 2026?

Sunrisers Eastern Cape won the 2026 SA20 title, their third championship in four seasons. They defeated Pretoria Capitals by 6 wickets in the final at Newlands, Cape Town. Matthew Breetzke scored 68 not out and Marco Jansen claimed 3 wickets for 28 runs in a match where Dewald Brevis of Pretoria Capitals became the first player to score a SA20 final century.

Which bookmakers are best for SA20 betting?

Betway is the official Betway SA20 league sponsor and carries the widest range of SA20 markets. Hollywoodbets, World Sports Betting, Sportingbet, and Gbets all cover SA20 with match winner, top batsman, top bowler, and tournament winner markets. Pre-tournament markets typically open 4-6 weeks before the first match.

How does the SA20 format work?

Each of the 6 teams plays the other 5 twice in the group stage, producing 30 matches in total. The top four qualify for the playoffs, which follow a two-qualifier structure. The two best sides play Qualifier 1, with the winner going directly to the final. The loser gets another shot in Qualifier 2 against the winner of the Eliminator (between 3rd and 4th). The winners of Qualifier 1 and Qualifier 2 meet in the final.
