FICA Speed Test: How Fast Do SA Bookmakers Verify You?

We registered accounts, submitted documents, and timed the results. Fastest: 12 hours. Slowest: 72 hours. Here's the full breakdown.

By Sipho Nkosi  |  Updated April 2026  |  18+ only

FICA verification is the step that stands between you and your withdrawal. Every licensed South African bookmaker must verify your identity under the Financial Intelligence Centre Act before paying out funds. The process is consistent in what it requires, but how fast operators process it varies a lot. We ran the test across 15 SA betting sites to get concrete numbers.

About This Test

We created real accounts at 15 SA betting sites and submitted standard FICA documents: a clear photo of a South African ID book or smart ID card, plus a bank statement less than three months old as proof of address. All documents were high-quality scans or photos taken in good lighting with no cut-off edges.

We recorded the time from document submission to the verification confirmation arriving, either by email or in-app notification. Tests were conducted across standard business days. Times do not reflect weekends or public holidays, where some operators may operate with reduced compliance teams.

Disclaimer: Times are estimates based on our testing and user reports. Actual processing times vary depending on document quality, operator workload, and whether your submission triggers a manual review. Your experience may differ.

Tips to Speed Up FICA Verification

Document quality is the single biggest factor you control. A blurry photo or a cropped edge sends your submission back to the queue. Read our full FICA guide for the complete process. The key steps:

1
Photograph in natural light. Avoid flash, which creates glare on laminated ID cards. Take the photo outdoors or near a bright window. The entire document must be in frame with visible edges.
2
Submit via app, not email. App and web portal submissions go directly into a structured review queue. Email submissions are often manually sorted before processing, which adds time.
3
Verify before you need to withdraw. Complete your FICA the day you register. If you wait until you have funds to withdraw, you're starting the clock at the worst possible moment.
4
Use a recent bank statement. The statement must be less than three months old and show your full name and current address. Redact your account number if you prefer, but the name and address must be clear.
5
Ensure your name matches exactly. The name on your ID must match the name used to register your betting account. Any discrepancy triggers a manual review and delays approval.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FICA verification at South African betting sites?

FICA stands for the Financial Intelligence Centre Act. SA betting sites are required by law to verify your identity before processing withdrawals. This typically involves uploading your ID document and a proof of address. Some operators also request a selfie.

How long does FICA verification take at SA betting sites?

Based on our testing, FICA verification at SA betting sites takes between 12 and 72 hours. Easybet and Lulabet were the fastest, completing verification within 12 to 24 hours. WSB and Betway took up to 72 hours in some cases.

Can I bet without completing FICA verification?

Yes, in most cases. SA bookmakers generally allow you to deposit and place bets without FICA verification. Verification is required before you can make a withdrawal. Some operators set a withdrawal threshold above which FICA becomes mandatory.

What documents do I need for FICA verification?

Most SA bookmakers require a South African ID document or valid passport, plus a proof of address less than three months old. Acceptable proof of address includes a utility bill, bank statement, or municipal rates notice. Some operators also accept a lease agreement.

Does Easybet give a bonus for completing FICA?

Yes. Easybet offers a R50 FICA bonus when you complete identity verification. This is one of the few SA operators that incentivises the process with a cash reward. Hollywoodbets offers a R25 verification bonus.
