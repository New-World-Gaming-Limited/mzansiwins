Betting in KwaZulu-Natal: Overview

KwaZulu-Natal has a rich sporting history and one of the most passionate betting communities in South Africa. The province is home to the Sharks, AmaZulu FC, Golden Arrows, and the Dolphins cricket franchise. The biggest single betting event of the SA calendar, the Hollywoodbets Durban July, takes place in Durban each July.

Hollywoodbets, founded in Durban in 1999, is the most recognisable bookmaker brand in KZN and remains one of the top five betting operators in the country. The operator has a physical presence across the province and offers one of the broadest selections of payment methods for KZN customers.

All 44 operators listed on this page accept bets from KwaZulu-Natal residents. Licensing is handled by the KwaZulu-Natal Gambling Board for KZN-based operators, though most operators use national-scope licences issued by other provincial boards.

KZN Gambling Board and Regulation

The KwaZulu-Natal Gambling Board regulates gambling within the province under the KwaZulu-Natal Gambling Act, No. 10 of 2010. The board licences casinos, limited payout machines, horse racing, bingo, and sports betting operations within KZN. Hollywoodbets and a handful of smaller operators hold or have held KZN-specific betting licences.

Under the National Gambling Act, a licensed operator from any province can take bets from KZN residents. This means you can legally use any of the 44 bookmakers listed here regardless of which province issued their licence.

KZN residents can self-exclude through the KZN Gambling Board's exclusion register or through the National Self-Exclusion database. If you want to take a break from betting, contact the SA Responsible Gambling Foundation on 0800 006 008 (free, 24/7).

Popular Sports for KZN Bettors

Rugby: The Sharks

The Sharks are based at Kings Park Stadium in Durban and compete in the URC and Champions Cup. They have one of the most loyal supporter bases in SA rugby. During the URC season, all 44 operators listed here offer Sharks match betting, including result, handicap, first try scorer, and total points markets. The Lions-Sharks and Bulls-Sharks derbies attract some of the strongest betting volumes in SA rugby.

Football: AmaZulu and Golden Arrows

AmaZulu FC, nicknamed Usuthu, play at Moses Mabhida Stadium and are one of the PSL's traditional clubs. They finished runners-up in the PSL in 2021 and reached the CAF Champions League. Golden Arrows are based in Durban and have a loyal following in KZN. Both clubs are covered in PSL betting markets at every operator on this page.

Cricket: The Dolphins

The Hollywoodbets Dolphins represent KZN in franchise cricket. They play home games at Hollywoodbets Kingsmead Cricket Stadium in Durban. Kingsmead also hosts Test matches and major ODI events. SA20 has brought the Durban's Super Giants to KZN, adding a new cricket team to bet on. Most operators cover CSA franchise cricket and SA20.

Horse Racing: The Durban July

Greyville Racecourse in Durban hosts the Hollywoodbets Durban July, the most celebrated horse race in Africa. Held each July, the event combines elite racing with fashion and social spectacle. It generates the largest single-day betting volumes in SA. Hollywoodbets, as naming sponsor, has the deepest Durban July markets. World Sports Betting, betXchange, and Supabets also offer strong horse racing coverage year-round at Greyville and Scottsville racecourses.

Durban July Betting Guide

The Hollywoodbets Durban July takes place at Greyville Racecourse, Durban, on the first Saturday of July each year. It is a Grade 1 flat race over 2,200 metres and attracts South Africa's top horses and trainers. The race draws over 50,000 spectators and millions of rands in betting.

Types of Durban July Bets

  • ✓Win bet: Back a horse to finish first. Available at fixed odds or through the tote pool.
  • ✓Each-way bet: Half on win, half on place (usually top 3). Good for horses at longer odds.
  • ✓Exacta: Predict the first and second finishers in correct order.
  • ✓Trifecta: Predict the first three finishers in correct order. Higher risk, big payouts.
  • ✓Place accumulator: Back multiple horses to place across races on the same card.

Tip: Hollywoodbets as naming sponsor usually offers the most Durban July specials and enhanced odds. Register early and check their racing section from mid-June when ante-post markets open.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is online betting legal in KwaZulu-Natal?

Yes. Online sports betting is legal for adults aged 18 and over across KwaZulu-Natal. The KwaZulu-Natal Gambling Board licences operators who want to take bets from KZN residents. All 44 operators on this page hold valid SA provincial gambling licences and accept bets from KZN bettors legally.

What is the Hollywoodbets Durban July?

The Hollywoodbets Durban July is South Africa's most prestigious horse racing event and one of the biggest sporting and social occasions on the SA calendar. Held at Greyville Racecourse in Durban every July, it attracts over 50,000 spectators and generates the largest horse racing betting volumes of the year. Hollywoodbets is the naming sponsor. Most SA bookmakers offer Durban July betting markets including win, place, each-way, and exotic bets.

How do I bet on the Durban July?

To bet on the Durban July, register with any of the 44 operators listed here, complete verification, and deposit funds. Look for the horse racing section and find the Durban July race card. You can bet on race winner (fixed odds or tote), each-way bets, quinellas, and exotics like trifectas. Hollywoodbets and World Sports Betting have the deepest Durban July markets. Odds are usually posted a week before the race.

Which KZN sports teams can I bet on?

The Sharks play URC and European club rugby and are one of SA's most popular betting teams. AmaZulu FC and Golden Arrows compete in the PSL. The Dolphins and Hollywoodbets Dolphins play in franchise cricket. All of these teams are available for betting at every licensed SA bookmaker.

Which payment methods work for KZN bettors?

Capitec, FNB, Standard Bank, Nedbank, and Absa are all accepted for EFT deposits. Ozow instant EFT is the fastest option, available at most operators. OTT Vouchers from Shoprite, Pick n Pay, and other retailers are widely used in Durban and KZN. Most bookmakers also accept 1Voucher and EasyLoad.

Does any bookmaker have a specific KZN licence?

Hollywoodbets, founded in Durban, holds a national sports betting licence and is the most identifiably KZN bookmaker. Most SA operators hold licences from the Western Cape, Mpumalanga, or Gauteng boards that are valid nationally. You can bet legally from any KZN location with any of the 44 operators listed here.
