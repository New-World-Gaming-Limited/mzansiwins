How Referral Bonuses Work

A refer-a-friend bonus is a two-sided incentive. You - the existing customer - earn a reward for every friend you bring to a bookmaker. Your friend also benefits, often landing an enhanced welcome offer alongside their standard signup bonus.

The mechanics are straightforward. Log into your account, find the referral programme under Promotions or My Account, and copy your unique referral link or code. Send it to your friend. When they complete the qualifying steps (usually registering and placing a first bet), the bookmaker credits both accounts with the agreed bonus.

Referral amounts shift regularly as operators adjust their promotional budgets. Rather than quoting specific figures that may already be outdated, the operator cards below link directly to each bookmaker's current promotions page. Check there for the latest offer before you start sharing links.

Step-by-Step: How to Refer a Friend

The process is similar across most SA bookmakers, though specific menu paths differ. Follow these general steps:

  1. 1
    Log in and find the referral section

    Go to your account dashboard. Look for "Refer a Friend", "Promotions", or "Share & Earn" - most bookmakers tuck it under the Promotions tab or in the account menu.

  2. 2
    Copy your unique referral link or code

    The bookmaker generates a link or code tied to your account. This is how they track who referred whom. Don't share a generic link from the homepage - it won't be attributed to you.

  3. 3
    Send the link to your friend

    Share via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Your friend must use your specific link to register - not a link from a different source. Make sure they know to click it before starting the sign-up process.

  4. 4
    Friend registers and completes the qualifying action

    Most operators require the new customer to register a real-money account, verify their identity (FICA), make a qualifying deposit, and place a bet meeting the minimum stake and odds. All these steps usually need to happen within a set time window.

  5. 5
    Both accounts receive the bonus

    Once the qualifying bet settles, the bookmaker credits both you and your friend. Timing varies - some operators credit within 24 hours, others within a few days. If the bonus doesn't appear, contact support with your friend's registered details.

Tips for Maximising Referral Bonuses

  • ✓Refer friends who genuinely want to bet. Referral bonuses only pay out after the friend completes qualifying conditions. Someone who signs up but never deposits wastes the opportunity for both of you.
  • ✓Match the bookmaker to your friend's interests. Refer a football fan to a site with the best soccer coverage. Refer a horse racing enthusiast to WSB or Hollywoodbets. The better the product fit, the more likely they'll place the qualifying bet quickly.
  • ✓Refer at the same time as a major sporting event. Friends are more likely to register and bet during a World Cup week, the Springboks season, or the PSL run-in. Timing your share around a fixture can cut the gap between sign-up and qualifying bet to a single day.
  • ✓Spread referrals across multiple bookmakers. Each programme operates independently. You can refer the same person to Betway, Hollywoodbets, and Supabets - they'll need separate accounts at each, but all three referral bonuses are in play. Compare all options on our betting sites page.
  • ✓Remind your friend about FICA verification. Most SA bookmakers require identity documents before a qualifying bet can be placed. Sending your friend to the FICA requirements page upfront avoids delays. Our FICA guide lists what's typically needed.
  • ✓Check for referral caps before you start. Many operators limit how many referral bonuses you can earn per month or in total. Read the T&Cs first so you're not surprised when the fifth referral doesn't pay out.

Referral Bonus Terms to Watch

Refer-a-friend bonuses look simple from the outside. The conditions attached to them are where things get complicated. Read these before sharing your link.

Term What it means Why it matters
Qualifying bet The bet the referred friend must place to trigger the bonus Minimum stake and minimum odds usually apply. A low-odds certainty bet often won't count.
Wagering requirement How many times you must turn over the bonus before withdrawing A 3x requirement on a R100 bonus means placing R300 in total bets before cashing out.
Referral cap Maximum number of referrals that pay out per account Many operators cap at 5 or 10 referrals per month. Check before referring large groups.
Validity window How long after registration the friend has to place the qualifying bet If the window is 30 days and your friend delays, the referral won't count.
Bonus expiry How long you have to use your own referral reward once it's credited Typically 7 to 30 days. Unused bonus credit is usually forfeited after expiry.
New customers only The referred person must have no prior account with the bookmaker Referring someone who already has an inactive account with the same operator won't qualify.

Terms change as operators update their promotions budgets, especially around major sporting events. Always read the current T&Cs on the operator's site before sharing links. For a broader look at betting bonus conditions, see our welcome bonus guide and the promo codes page.

Referral Bonus FAQ

What is a referral bonus at a betting site?

A referral bonus - also called a refer-a-friend bonus - rewards existing customers for inviting friends to join a bookmaker. When the friend registers through your unique link and completes a qualifying bet, both of you receive a bonus, usually a free bet or bonus credit.

Which SA betting sites have a refer-a-friend programme?

Several SA-licensed bookmakers run refer-a-friend programmes, including Betway, Hollywoodbets, Supabets, World Sports Betting, and Easybet. Specific amounts change frequently - always check the promotions page of your chosen operator for the current terms before sharing links.

How does the referral process work?

Log into your account and find the refer-a-friend section under Promotions or My Account. Copy your unique referral link or code, share it with your friend, and they register using that link. Once they place a qualifying bet, both accounts receive the agreed bonus.

Do both people get a bonus?

Usually yes. Most SA bookmakers reward both the referrer and the newly registered friend. The referrer typically receives a free bet or bonus credit after the friend's qualifying bet settles. The friend may also receive a welcome offer on top of any referral benefit.

Is there a limit on how many friends I can refer?

Most operators cap the number of referrals per account - sometimes per month, sometimes in total. The cap varies by bookmaker. Check the specific T&Cs before you start so you know exactly how many referral rewards are available to you.

What happens if my friend doesn't complete the qualifying bet?

If your friend registers but doesn't place a qualifying bet within the required window - or bets below the minimum stake or on an excluded market - neither party receives the referral bonus. The conditions must be met in full for the payout to trigger.

Can I refer the same friend to multiple bookmakers?

Yes. Refer-a-friend programmes are operator-specific and run independently. You can refer the same person to Betway, Hollywoodbets, and Supabets at the same time. Your friend needs a new account at each bookmaker to qualify at each one.

Do referral bonuses have wagering requirements?

Most do. Referral free bets and bonus credits usually come with wagering requirements before winnings can be withdrawn. The multiplier varies by operator and offer. Always read the T&Cs before claiming - the key thing to check is the rollover amount and the minimum odds that qualify.
