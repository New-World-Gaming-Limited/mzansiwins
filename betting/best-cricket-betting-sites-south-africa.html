Cricket Betting Markets Explained

Cricket is one of the deepest betting sports available at SA bookmakers. Beyond the basic match-winner, you can bet on individual player performance, session runs, and specific dismissal methods. The variety of formats, T20, ODI, and Test, means the market structure changes with each type of game.

  • Match Winner Pick which team wins. In T20s this is straightforward. In Test matches you also have the option to back a draw.
  • Top Batsman Pick the highest scorer in an innings or match from the list the bookmaker provides.
  • Top Bowler Pick the bowler who takes the most wickets in an innings or match.
  • Total Runs Bet over or under the bookmaker's line for total runs in an innings or match.
  • Man of the Match Pick the player awarded the match award. High risk, but can pay well in a multi-bet.
  • Method of Dismissal Pick how a named batsman gets out, bowled, caught, LBW, run out, or stumped.
  • Session Runs Wager on runs scored in a defined session, such as the first 10 overs or the morning session in a Test.

T20 cricket produces the most liquid in-play markets. Odds shift quickly after boundaries and wickets, so have your bets prepared before the over begins if you're playing in-play.

SA20 Betting Guide

The SA20 is South Africa's premier T20 franchise league, launched in 2023. Six teams compete at venues across the country each January and February, with broadcasters and bookmakers giving it near-Premier League treatment in terms of market depth.

The Six SA20 Teams

  • Sunrisers Eastern Cape Based in Gqeberha, Sunrisers are the defending champions with a balanced squad.
  • MI Cape Town Mumbai Indians' Cape Town franchise, playing at Newlands.
  • Joburg Super Kings Chennai Super Kings' SA franchise, based at the Wanderers.
  • Paarl Royals Rajasthan Royals' franchise, playing at Boland Park.
  • Durban's Super Giants Based at Kingsmead, strong local support base.
  • Pretoria Capitals Playing at SuperSport Park in Centurion.

Bookmakers typically offer 15 to 20 pre-match markets per SA20 fixture, expanding to live in-play markets during the game. Worth looking at: top-run scorer across the tournament, winner of each conference group, and head-to-head win markets.

IPL and International Cricket

The Indian Premier League draws huge betting volumes globally, and SA bookmakers are no exception. Expect full pre-match and in-play coverage from the first ball. The T20 World Cup, held every two years, is the biggest short-format international event and typically prompts enhanced odds and specials at sites like Betway and 10Bet.

Test match betting requires a different approach. With games lasting five days, the draw is a legitimate result and odds move dramatically session by session. ODI cricket sits between the two formats, typically 50-over games with clear favourites based on home advantage and current form.

For ICC tournaments, check the ante-post market early. Tournament winner odds improve as the event draws closer, but pre-tournament prices can offer value before squads are confirmed and conditions are understood.

Live Cricket Betting

In-play cricket betting is available at Betway, Tictacbets, Easybet, and 10Bet on major fixtures. Markets update between balls rather than ball by ball in most cases, though some sites offer faster refreshes during T20 innings.

Common live markets include next wicket method, next dismissal, current over runs, and innings runs. Cash-out is available at Betway and Easybet on selected live cricket bets, allowing you to lock in a profit or cut a loss before the innings ends.

Cricket Betting Tips

Pitch Conditions

A dry, dusty pitch in Johannesburg will assist spinners by the third day of a Test. A green seamer in Durban rewards fast bowlers. Read the pitch report before betting on total runs or method of dismissal markets.

Toss Impact in T20s

The toss matters significantly in T20 cricket. Teams winning the toss often choose to field first in day-night games. Check the toss result before placing live in-play bets, chasing a modest target is statistically favourable in T20 conditions.

Head-to-Head Records

Some teams consistently dominate certain opponents at specific venues. Betway's statistics section and third-party cricket data sites like ESPN Cricinfo show venue-specific head-to-head records worth checking before placing match-winner bets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is cricket betting legal in South Africa?

Yes. Cricket betting is legal at any bookmaker that holds a valid provincial gambling licence. All sites listed on MzansiWins are licenced under the National Gambling Act.

Which sites offer the best SA20 odds?

Betway, 10Bet, and Hollywoodbets consistently post competitive SA20 odds. Check each fixture across two or three sites before placing a bet.

Can I bet on the Proteas live?

Yes. Live (in-play) cricket betting is available at most SA bookmakers, including Betway, Tictacbets, and 10Bet. Markets refresh ball by ball.

What is a top-batsman bet?

A top-batsman market asks you to pick which batter will score the most runs in a specific innings. You pick from a list of players at the bookmaker's offered odds.

Do bookmakers offer IPL betting in South Africa?

Yes. The IPL is widely covered at SA bookmakers. Expect match-winner, top batsman, top bowler, total sixes, and in-play markets from the opening fixture.

What is the man of the match market?

You pick the player awarded man of the match after the game ends. It's a higher-risk, higher-reward bet that works well combined with a match-winner in a multi-bet.

How does session betting work in cricket?

Session betting lets you wager on the number of runs scored in a specific session, such as the first 10 overs. You pick over or under the bookmaker's session run line.

What is method of dismissal betting?

You bet on how a named batsman will be dismissed, bowled, caught, LBW, run out, or stumped. Betway and 10Bet offer this market on major fixtures.
