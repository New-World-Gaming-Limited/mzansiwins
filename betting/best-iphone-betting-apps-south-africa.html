How to Download a Betting App on iPhone

All apps listed above are available directly from the Apple App Store. No APK installation or changing security settings is needed.

  • Step 1: Open the App Store Tap the App Store icon on your iPhone home screen.
  • Step 2: Search Type the bookmaker name (e.g. Betway or Hollywoodbets) in the search bar.
  • Step 3: Check the publisher Confirm the app is from the official operator. Look at the Developer name on the App Store listing.
  • Step 4: Download Tap Get and authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
  • Step 5: Register or log in Open the app. New users register an account. Existing users log in with their credentials.
  • Step 6: Deposit Navigate to the Deposit or Cashier section, choose your payment method, and add funds to start betting.

Enable push notifications when the app asks, you'll receive match alerts, odds boosts, and bonus notifications. You can customise notification types in your iPhone settings under the app's notification preferences.

iPhone App Features to Look For

The best iPhone betting apps share a common set of features that make mobile betting faster and more secure.

Face ID and Touch ID Login

Face ID and Touch ID let you log in instantly without typing a password. All apps in our top rankings support biometric login on compatible iPhone models.

Push Notifications

Notifications alert you to match kick-offs, odds changes, and bonus activations. Enable them after installation and customise in iOS settings to avoid notification overload.

Apple Pay Support

Betway, Easybet, and Zarbet accept Apple Pay for deposits. Apple Pay transactions are processed instantly and never share your card number with the bookmaker.

Widgets

Some apps including Betway support iOS home screen widgets showing live scores or upcoming fixtures. Check the app's Today View additions in your iPhone's widget edit menu.

iPhone vs Android Betting Apps

The main difference between iPhone and Android betting in South Africa is how apps are distributed.

App Store vs APK

iPhone apps must go through Apple's App Store review process. This means every iOS betting app has been checked by Apple before you can install it. Android apps in South Africa are often distributed as APK files directly from the operator site, bypassing the Google Play Store.

Updates

iOS apps update automatically through the App Store. Android APK apps may require you to manually download and install the new version when the operator releases an update.

Security

Both platforms are secure when you download from official sources. On iPhone, the App Store is the only install route for most users, which simplifies the verification process.

If you use both Apple and Android devices, check whether the bookmaker offers consistent account functionality across platforms. Betway, Easybet, and Hollywoodbets all maintain near-identical feature sets on iOS and Android.

Tips for Betting on iPhone

  • Enable notifications selectively Turn on match start alerts and bonus notifications, but consider turning off generic promotional pings to avoid fatigue.
  • Set deposit limits in the app All licensed SA bookmakers offer responsible gambling tools in the app. Set your monthly deposit limit before placing your first bet.
  • Use WiFi for live betting Live in-play betting requires a fast connection to keep pace with rapidly changing odds. Switch to WiFi when available for the most stable experience.
  • Check the App Store rating before installing The App Store rating reflects real user experience. Anything below 3.5 stars warrants checking reviews for patterns around withdrawals or crashes.

Apple Pay Support on SA Betting Apps

Apple Pay deposits clear instantly and use Face ID or Touch ID for confirmation, which is quicker than entering card details every time.

Operators that support Apple Pay (May 2026)

Betway, Easybet, and Zarbet support Apple Pay deposits. The fee is the same as a regular card deposit (free at all three) and the funds appear instantly.

How Apple Pay works at SA bookmakers

Add your card to Wallet first (this only needs to be done once). When depositing, choose Apple Pay, double-tap the side button, and confirm with Face ID. The deposit is instant and the operator never sees your real card number , Apple Pay generates a one-time token.

Withdrawal back to Apple Pay

No SA bookmaker supports withdrawal back to Apple Pay. Withdrawals always go to EFT or Ozow Payout regardless of how you deposited. This is a card-network limitation, not an operator choice.

Face ID, Touch ID, and iPhone Security

Biometric login is faster, safer, and removes the most common reason people get locked out of their account: forgotten passwords.

Setting up Face ID for a betting app

After your first password login, the app prompts you to enable Face ID. If you missed the prompt, go to the app's Settings or Account section and look for "Login Settings" or "Biometric Login". Enable it and confirm with Face ID once. The next time you launch the app, Face ID logs you in automatically.

What about shared devices?

Do not enable biometric login on a shared iPhone. Anyone with their face registered to that phone could access your account. Use a strong password instead, or set up the app to require Face ID every time even after login.

App Lock (extra security layer)

Several apps including 10Bet and Betway offer an in-app PIN that activates whenever the app comes to the foreground. This is independent of the iOS unlock and protects against the rare case where someone has access to an unlocked phone but you do not want them to see your betting account.

Live Streaming on iPhone

Live streaming of football, racing, and tennis is increasingly common on SA betting apps but availability varies by event and operator.

Apps with live streaming on iOS (May 2026)

Betway , Premier League, La Liga, ATP tennis, selected racing. Hollywoodbets , every Hollywoodbets-sponsored race plus most international racing. 10Bet , selected football and tennis. Streaming is free for funded accounts and uses minimal data , about 200MB per hour at standard quality.

When live streaming counts as a "live bet"

Streaming requires you to either have a funded account or have a bet placed on the event. The exact rule varies by operator. At Betway, simply being logged in and funded is enough. At 10Bet you usually need an active bet on the market you want to watch.

Picture-in-Picture support

iOS supports Picture-in-Picture for video. The Betway app uses it: tap the small PiP icon while streaming and the video shrinks to a corner of your screen so you can place bets in the same app or browse other apps. Hollywoodbets does not currently use PiP.

Common iPhone App Issues and Fixes

App will not download from App Store

Check your country setting in App Store > Profile > Country/Region. SA betting apps are only available in the South Africa store. If you are signed into a different region, switch to the SA App Store first.

App crashes immediately after opening

Force-close the app (swipe up, swipe up on the app card), then relaunch. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall. Your account data is server-side, so reinstalling does not lose anything.

Push notifications not arriving

Settings > [App Name] > Notifications. Make sure "Allow Notifications" is on, plus any specific categories like Match Start or Bet Settled. The app may also have its own notification preferences , check the in-app settings as well.

Apple Pay not showing as a deposit option

Apple Pay only appears if your iCloud is signed in and you have at least one card in Wallet. Check Settings > Wallet & Apple Pay first. The bookmaker must also support Apple Pay (currently Betway, Easybet, and Zarbet on the SA market).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download a betting app on my iPhone?

Open the App Store, search for the bookmaker's name (e.g. Betway or Hollywoodbets), tap Get, and install. Log in or register. All apps listed here are available directly from the App Store with no sideloading.

Are SA betting apps safe to download from the App Store?

Yes. Apple reviews all App Store submissions before publishing. Every app listed here is from a licensed SA operator. Look for the official publisher name on the App Store listing to confirm it's the right app.

Which iPhone betting app has the best live streaming?

Betway and Hollywoodbets both offer live streaming on selected events from their iOS apps. You need a funded account to access the stream.

Do iPhone betting apps support Apple Pay?

Betway, Easybet, and Zarbet support Apple Pay for deposits on iPhone. Check the payments section in the app after registering to confirm current support.

Can I use Face ID or Touch ID to log in to betting apps?

Yes. Most modern SA betting apps including Betway, 10Bet, and Easybet support biometric login on iPhone models with Face ID or Touch ID. Enable it in the app settings after first login.

Do betting apps work on older iPhones?

Lulabet and Hollywoodbets are notably lightweight and work well on older iPhones (iPhone 7 or later). Betway and 10Bet require iOS 14 or newer, check the App Store listing for minimum requirements.

Will my bonus work if I register through the iPhone app?

Yes. Welcome bonuses apply regardless of whether you register through the app or the website, as long as you use the correct promo code. The code is listed on each brand card above.

How do I withdraw winnings through the iPhone app?

Go to the Payments or Cashier section in the app, select Withdraw, choose your payment method (EFT, Ozow, or card where supported), enter the amount, and confirm. Processing times vary by method, EFT typically takes 1 to 3 business days.
