What Is 1Voucher?

1Voucher (also called 1ForYou) is a prepaid voucher system built for South African consumers. You pay cash at a retailer, get a 16-digit PIN in return, and use that PIN to fund online accounts without ever sharing bank details. The voucher provider processes everything through its own secure network.

For casino deposits specifically, this matters. You control exactly how much you load, because you can only spend what's on the voucher. There's no automatic debit, no card charge, and no way for a casino to access your bank account. That combination of privacy and hard spending limits is why 1Voucher has become one of the most-used deposit methods at South African online casinos.

Vouchers come in fixed denominations starting at R5 and go up to R4,000 per voucher. You can buy multiple vouchers and combine them within a single casino deposit session at supported sites. The balance you don't use stays in your 1ForYou digital wallet and can be redeemed later.

How to Deposit with 1Voucher at SA Casinos

The process is the same across every operator that accepts 1Voucher. From buying the voucher to funds appearing in your casino account takes less than two minutes.

Visit any Shoprite, Checkers, Pick n Pay, PEP, Ackermans, or Flash-enabled store. Tell the cashier the denomination you want, pay cash, and receive a printed slip with your 16-digit PIN. You can also buy online at 1voucher.co.za or through the 1ForYou app.

2

Log into your casino account

Open the casino site or app on your phone or browser. Sign in with your username and password. If you haven't registered yet, the sign-up process takes about 3 minutes.

3

Open the cashier and select 1Voucher

Navigate to Deposit or Wallet in the casino menu. Look for "1Voucher" or "1ForYou" in the payment method list. On mobile apps it's usually listed under Vouchers or Prepaid.

4

Enter your PIN and deposit amount

Type the 16-digit voucher PIN into the cashier field. Enter the amount you want to deposit. This must be equal to or less than the voucher value. Some sites let you split the amount across a partial spend, leaving the rest in your 1ForYou wallet.

5

Confirm the transaction

Tap or click Deposit. The funds appear in your casino balance instantly. No waiting, no SMS confirmation from the bank. You'll see a transaction reference on screen and the casino will often send a confirmation email.

Tip: Keep your voucher slip until the deposit confirms. If there's a technical issue you'll need the PIN to retry. Once used successfully, the PIN is deactivated.
