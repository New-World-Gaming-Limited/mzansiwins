How it works

Convert bookmaker odds into percentage probabilities to understand what the market thinks the true chance of an outcome is.

Quick Example

Example: Betway has Springboks to beat Australia at 1.45.
Calculation: 1 / 1.45 x 100 = 68.97%
The market implies the Springboks have roughly a 69% chance of winning. If you believe the true probability is higher than 69%, the bet may offer value.
