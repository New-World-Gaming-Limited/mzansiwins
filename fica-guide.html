FICA Verification Guide for SA Bettors

FICA verification is a legal requirement for all licensed South African betting operators. Before processing a withdrawal, operators must verify your identity under the Financial Intelligence Centre Act. This page explains what documents are needed and how the process works.

What is FICA?

FICA stands for the Financial Intelligence Centre Act. All licensed South African betting operators are required to verify your identity before processing withdrawals. This protects against fraud and money laundering, and applies to every operator listed on this site.

Documents You Need

  • South African ID - green book, smart ID card, or valid passport. Smart ID cards are typically processed fastest.
  • Proof of address - utility bill, bank statement, or municipal account dated within the last 3 months.
  • Proof of payment method - screenshot or photo of the bank account or e-wallet you deposit with.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Register Your Account

Register using the name exactly as it appears on your ID document. Name mismatches between your account and ID are the most common cause of verification delays.

Step 2: Upload Documents

Most bookmakers have a "Verify Account" or "FICA" section in your profile. Upload clear photos or scans. All text must be readable and no corners cut off. Blurry or partially obscured images are the most common reason for rejection.

Step 3: Wait for Approval

Typically 24 to 48 hours. Hollywoodbets and Betway tend to process faster, sometimes within a few hours during business hours. Other operators may take the full 48 hours.

Step 4: Start Withdrawing

Once verified, you can withdraw to any linked payment method. The first withdrawal may take slightly longer as operators run a final check. Subsequent withdrawals are typically faster.

Tips for Fast Verification

  • Use your smart ID card - it processes faster than the old green book
  • Take photos in good lighting with a plain background
  • Make sure your proof of address is from the last 3 months
  • Upload during SA business hours (8am to 5pm) for the fastest turnaround
  • If you have not heard back after 48 hours, contact their support team

Common Issues

Name mismatch: Your registration name must match your ID exactly. If you registered with a different name, contact support to correct it before uploading documents.

Blurry documents: The most common reason for rejection. Take clear, well-lit photos where all text is fully legible.

Expired proof of address: Must be within 3 months. If you do not have a recent one, download a bank statement from your banking app. Takes 30 seconds.
